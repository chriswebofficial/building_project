Oat Construction Nigeria Limited is an indigenous construction company specializing in building and civil engineering projects. We have the capability to handle diverse designs in building, civil engineering, and related fields.

About us

Our company is experiencing rapid growth, fueled by our unwavering commitment to delivering projects with the highest level of skill and efficiency. We constantly renew our dedication to ensure that we are well-equipped to serve our clients effectively, delivering projects within budget and on time while ensuring value for money and return on investment.

We offer a comprehensive range of services including building and civil engineering, energy, water and wastewater treatment, environmental protection, project development, and real estate.

Through the strategic deployment of technology and skills, we have earned a reputation for maintaining certified processes and meeting rigorous standards in project procurement, execution, and delivery, regardless of project size. We are proud to be ISO 9001-2008 compliant and certified.

Our approach to client relationships is characterized by responsiveness, empathy, and flexibility. We are committed to understanding and aligning with our clients’ objectives and preferences, and we are adaptable in structuring contractual agreements to suit their needs.

Above all, our dedication to client satisfaction serves as the driving force behind our work. We approach every project with enthusiasm and determination, striving for excellence in execution, particularly in turnkey projects where we are engaged from conception to completion.
